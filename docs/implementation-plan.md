# Ke hoach Trien khai: Du an Pet Grooming V2 (AI-First)

Du an tap trung vao viec nang cap landing page thanh mot nen tang dich vu cham soc thu cung thong minh, tich hop cong nghe AI de toi ưu hoa trai nghiem nguoi dung va he thong quan tri Admin.

---

## Cong nghe Su dung (Tech Stack)

- **Frontend:** React.js (Vite), Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion.
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js.
- **Database:** MongoDB (Mongoose).
- **AI Recognition:** TensorFlow.js (Client-side inference).
- **Chatbot:** Google Gemini API (Free Tier).
- **Authentication:** JSON Web Token (JWT).
- **Thanh toan:** Stripe hoac PayPal (Sandbox mode).

---

## Phan chia Trach nhiem (4 Thanh vien)

### 1. Thanh vien 1: Frontend Specialist
**Linh vuc: Giao dien nguoi dung (UI/UX) & Trai nghiem tuong tac.**
- **He thong Design:** Cap nhat tailwind.config.js theo phong cach Joyful Clay-Cloud.
- **Quy trinh dat lich (Booking Wizard):** Xay dung quy trinh 3 buoc voi hieu ung chuyen canh.
- **Tich hop UI AI:** Phat trien component tai len va hieu ung quet anh (scanning effect).
- **Dashboard Admin:** Thiet ke giao dien quan tri va bao cao thong ke doanh thu/don hang.

### 2. Thanh vien 2: Backend Specialist
**Linh vuc: Xay dung he thong loi va API.**
- **API Core:** Xay dung Express API cho cac chuc nang CRUD (Dat lich, Thu cung, Dich vu).
- **Logic Nghiep vu:** Thiet lap luong du lieu ket noi giua AI ket qua va goi dich vu tuong ung.
- **He thong thong bao:** Tich hop Nodemailer de gui email thong bao tu dong.

### 3. Thanh vien 3: AI & Database Architect
**Linh vuc: Tri tue nhan tao va Cau truc du lieu.**
- **Nhan dien thu cung (Pet Recognition):** Su dung TensorFlow.js de trien khai mo hinh nhan dien loai va giong thu cung ngay tren trinh duyet.
- **Chatbot ho tro:** Tich hop Google Gemini API de xay dung tro ly ao tu van cham soc thu cung.
- **Thiet ke Database:** Toi ưu hoa Schema MongoDB cho cac thuc the User, Pet, Appointment, va Payment.

### 4. Thanh vien 4: Security, Auth & Payment
**Linh vuc: Bao mat, Xac thuc va Giao dich.**
- **Xac thuc (Authentication):** Trien khai JWT cho he thong dang nhap giua User va Admin.
- **Quan tri Admin:** Xử ly logic nghiep vu backend cho Dashboard (duyet lich hen, quan ly doanh thu).
- **Thanh toan:** Tich hop cong thanh toan Stripe hoac PayPal (Sandbox) de xu ly giao dich.
